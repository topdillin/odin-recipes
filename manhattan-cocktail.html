Manhattan Cocktail

In the pantheon of drinks named after cities and regions (Singapore Sling, Moscow Mule, Brooklyn, Long Island Iced Tea), few have as much history and pedigree as the venerable Manhattan.

Like most drinks created in the 19th century, the recorded origins of the Manhattan cocktail waver between tall tales, hearsay, urban legends and a few hints of truth.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rye whiskey
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Brandied cherry, for garnish

Steps

  1. In mixing glass filled with ice, add all ingredients except garnish.
  2. Stir for 30â€“45 seconds, until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into chilled coupe glass.
  4. Garnish with brandied cherry.
